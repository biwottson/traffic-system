About Us

Welcome to the National Transport and Safety Authority (NTSA), the driving force behind a safer, more efficient road transport sector in Kenya. Established by the National Transport and Safety Authority Act (2012), NTSA plays a pivotal role in shaping road safety policies, implementing strategic programs, and ensuring the seamless operation of transport services.

Our Mission

At NTSA, our mission is clear: to safeguard road safety and enhance the overall efficiency of transportation services. We are dedicated to providing expert advice, implementing effective policies, and regulating the road transport sector to ensure the delivery of safe, reliable, and efficient public transport services.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Comprehensive Oversight: NTSA oversees crucial functions, including Motor Vehicle Registration, Transport Licensing, Motor Vehicle Inspection, Road Safety, Driver Testing, and Traffic Law enhancement.

  • Strategic Planning: We actively engage in planning, managing, and regulating the road transport sector. Our goal is to create and enforce standards that ensure compliance with road transport laws and regulations.

  • Innovation through Technology: The Traffic Offense Reporting System is a testament to our commitment to leveraging technology for positive change. By embracing a computerized system, we aim to transform the way traffic offenses are documented, managed, and analyzed.

Location

Our physical presence is rooted in Hill Park Building, Upper Hill, Nairobi, Kenya, but our impact extends across the nation.
